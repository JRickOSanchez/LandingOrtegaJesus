Favorite movies and series

This are my Favorite movies and series

My favorite movies

Sam Raimi's Spider-Man trilogy

Spider-Man directed by Sam Raimi and starring Tobey Maguire are one of my favorite movie sagas, because besides being Spider-Man my favorite super hero, they are movies that marked my childhood. Besides, in spite of telling you the trajectory that our character takes in the movies, it always ends up giving us multiple meaningful messages, such as: take responsibility for your actions, deal with loss, it is always good to be willing to ask for help, etc.

Joker

Joker is a movie that I like a lot, because it is a movie based on the super hero comics, very different from how we had raised for years and other productions of the same, giving a darker and "realistic" perspective on how a person can reach the breaking point or even to the same madness. This film is a social criticism of how the same people within a society can be the triggers or the cause of someone despite wanting to take the right path or the right decisions pushes them and decides them directly to want to take dark actions giving birth to people like the protagonist of this film.

Spider-Man in to the spider-verse

Spider-Man in to the spider-verse is one of my favorite animated movies, because it tells the origin of a version of spider-man not as well known as Miles Morales at that time. Besides the animation style that was revolutionary and very much in the style of the comics, it gives us some messages that are very important in its great story, it makes it clear that we can all be a spider-man in the metaphorical sense, since we all have problems and responsibilities with which we have to deal, and the answer to that is "no matter how many times I fall, I will always get up and keep fighting" and also "you never know when you'll be ready, life is a leap of faith and you always have to trust in yourself and your abilities".

My favorite series

Invincible

Invincible, based on the comics of the same name is an animated series aimed at an adult audience. Invincible could easily have been little more than a parody of other superheroes, in many of those who appear in the series could not be more evident which is the original model, also including gore in the series. For me, it is one of the best superhero series of recent years is that it also still has a lot of story to tell in future seasons.

Malcolm in the middle

Malcolm in the middle is a comedy series where it tells the story of a dysfunctional family like Malcolm's, this series has several comic hisotrias and even messages that help the characters grow. I really like this series mainly because of the comedy and the situations the characters get into.
